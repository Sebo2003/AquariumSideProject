Dive into a world of Fun with Reef Keeping!

Welcome to the world of reef keeping!

For all of my life, I've been entranced by the splendor of the sea. Funny, given I live hundreds of miles from the nearest sea. Nonetheless, through scuba diving and some angling, I've always kept this fascination close to heart. In fact, I kept it close to home! Keeping a coral reef in your home is extremley challenging - but its just as rewarding.

Explore A Whole New World!

I am very passionaite about anyting marine, and I want to to share this passion with anyone who would listen! This site lists just about everything I personally beleive will make any reef tank successful, but I cerntainly didn't list everything that there is to this hobby (especially on the livestock side of things - only so much time in a day after all).

Hopefully, this website will make clear just how much thought, maintance, and money goes into every system! What goes well together, how to repair broken equipment, logisitcs of placing the tank itself - it all most be well planned ahead of time to ensure success! Now, I am by no means an expert, but I've been around aquaria long enough to speak confidently on these issues.

Let's explore the reef together!
