It Ain't Fishy - Let's Learn About Fish!

Arguably the most exciting part of keeping a coral reef tank are the fish! Saltwater fish are known for their vibrant coloration and expressive personalities, and are, in my opinion, a lot more interesting than your freshwater stock. Of course, since we are putting live animals in a box - some fish get along together better than others (don't put predator and prey in the same box!). There are many things to consider when buying fish, but I've decided that the fish's size, minimum tank size, diet, aggressiveness, and their tendency to eat corals will be chieftain among them.
